Features

Everything you need to manage projects

Powerful features designed to help your team collaborate effectively and deliver projects on time.

Kanban Boards

Visualize your workflow with intuitive drag-and-drop Kanban boards. Move tasks seamlessly across customizable columns.

Real-Time Collaboration

Work together in real-time with instant updates. See changes as they happen with WebSocket-powered live sync.

Secure Credentials

Store project credentials safely with role-based access control. API keys, hosting details, and secrets in one secure place.

Smart Notifications

Never miss an update with intelligent notifications. Get alerts for assignments, mentions, due dates, and more.

Team Management

Manage roles, permissions, and invitations with ease. Admin controls give you full visibility over your team.

Advanced Filtering

Find anything instantly with powerful search and filters. Filter by assignee, due date, labels, status, and more.

Project Categories

Organize projects into categories for better management. Group by client, department, or any structure that works for you.

File Attachments

Attach files, images, and documents directly to tasks. Keep all project assets organized in one place.

Due Dates & Deadlines

Set due dates for tasks and never miss a deadline. Get automatic reminders before tasks are due.

Activity History

Track every change with detailed activity logs. See who did what and when for complete transparency.

Priority Labels

Mark tasks as urgent, high, medium, or low priority. Visual indicators help teams focus on what matters most.

Email Invitations

Invite team members via email with one click. New users can join projects instantly without complicated onboarding.
